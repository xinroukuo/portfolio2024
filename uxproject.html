Grocerteria

Groceries-located APP case study

Project Overview

Problem

Users who need a easy way to find products when they shop the grocery in person.

Goal

Design an app will let users find products in grocery which will affect users who needs to shop the grocery in person.

User Research

I Conducted user research by understanding users’ needs and frustrations with emphasize exercize, further creating persona for a group of potential users, organizing their pain points and mapping their journey of shopping local grocery in person.

Pain Points

Disabilities

Visual impairment and dyslexia

Health

Prepare meals for family in person

Budget

Save money on food and transportation

Time

Under busy and unpredictable work shifts

Persona

Reena

Age：36

Education：College

Hometown：Taipei

Family：Often live alone

Occupation：Paramedic

“Shopping in person is the most happiest thing except when something hard to find  ”

Goals

  • Easy to find products when shopping grocery in person
  • Buy both healthy and cost-effective products 

    • Frustrations

  • Dyslexia, hard to read text quickly
  • Conscious about health and budget

    • Reena is a paramedic who lives in Taipei. She often lives alone but shares custody of her daughter with her ex-partner. Because Reena needs to prepare meals for her daughter, she takes much time shop grocery to buy ingredients. She desires a good service to help her find products in grocery when shopping in person.
